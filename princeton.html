About Us

Innovation is the life force of society. Today, we can use technology and the web to shop, entertain, learn, and communicate across the globe in seconds.

So why not use this same technology to enable rapid, efficient, and inexpensive medical diagnosis for people in need of such services?

At Aurora that is exactly our aim!

From just one click away, any person in need of quick and reliable medical information can access our service, and speak directly to our site with his/her voice.

Just tell us how you feel, and what your major symptoms are. Then, our site will quickly provide you with links to reliable medical sites that relate to your symptoms and bridge the gap between you and the complexity of medicine.

Support us to help make medicine and medical diagnostics simpler, quicker, and easier for all people everywhere!
